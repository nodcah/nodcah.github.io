What does the WHTA do?

Encourages and represents West Hartford Taxpayers to be advocates
Encourages efficient and cost based operations
Encourages town government to provide needed services at fair cost

We are offering this web site as a place for people to get information about how well West Hartford is working, exchange information, educate themselves about local issues, and engage in a lively discussion about the future of West Hartford.

Please Join Us In This Endeavor.
